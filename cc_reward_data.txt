CREDIT CARD REWARDS DATA - Research Compiled December 1, 2025
================================================================

1. CITI DOUBLE CASH
-------------------
Earning Structure:
- 2% cash back total: 1% when you buy + 1% when you pay
- 5% total cash back on hotels, car rentals, and attractions booked through Citi Travel (3% additional + 2% base)
- All other purchases: 2% cash back

Notes:
- No annual fee
- Cash back earned as ThankYou Points (1 point = 1 cent)
- Must pay at least minimum payment on time to earn the 1% payback portion


2. CITI CUSTOM CASH
-------------------
Earning Structure:
- 5% cash back on highest eligible spend category each billing cycle (up to $500 spent)
- Eligible 5% categories: Restaurants, Gas Stations, Grocery Stores, Select Travel, Select Transit, Select Streaming Services, Drugstores, Home Improvement Stores, Fitness Clubs, Live Entertainment
- 5% cash back on hotels, car rentals, and attractions through Citi Travel (4% additional + 1% base)
- 1% cash back on all other purchases
- 1% cash back after exceeding $500 in top category

Notes:
- No annual fee
- Automatically awards 5% on highest category - no activation needed
- Cash back earned as ThankYou Points
- Special travel offer through 6/30/2026: additional 4% on Citi Travel bookings


3. CITI STRATA PREMIER
----------------------
Earning Structure:
- 10x points on hotels, car rentals, and attractions booked through Citi Travel
- 3x points on air travel and other hotel purchases
- 3x points at restaurants
- 3x points at supermarkets
- 3x points at gas stations and EV charging
- 1x point on all other purchases

Notes:
- $95 annual fee
- No foreign transaction fees
- $100 annual hotel credit (single stay of $500+ through Citi Travel)
- Points transfer to airline/hotel partners at 1:1 ratio


4. CITI STRATA (No Annual Fee)
-------------------------------
Earning Structure:
- 5x points on hotels, car rentals, and attractions booked through Citi Travel
- 3x points at supermarkets
- 3x points on select transit purchases
- 3x points at gas and EV charging stations
- 3x points on eligible self-select category (changeable once per quarter)
  Self-select categories: Fitness Clubs, Select Streaming Services, Live Entertainment, Cosmetic Stores/Barber Shops/Hair Salons, Pet Supply Stores
- 2x points at restaurants
- 1x point on all other purchases

Notes:
- No annual fee
- Default self-select category is Select Streaming Services
- Points transfer to partners at varying ratios (less favorable than Premier)


5. BILT MASTERCARD
------------------
Earning Structure:
- 1x point on rent payments (up to 100,000 points per calendar year, no transaction fee)
- 3x points on dining
- 2x points on travel (booked directly with airlines, hotels, or through Bilt portal)
- 1x point on all other purchases
- 5x points on Lyft rides (when accounts are linked)
- Rent Day bonus: Double points on non-rent purchases on 1st of each month (up to 1,000 bonus points)

Notes:
- No annual fee
- Must make at least 5 transactions per statement period to earn points
- New cardholders: 2x on all purchases for first 30 days


6. CAPITAL ONE VENTURE X
-------------------------
Earning Structure:
- 10x miles on hotels and rental cars booked through Capital One Travel
- 5x miles on flights and vacation rentals booked through Capital One Travel
- 2x miles on all other purchases
- 5x miles on Capital One Entertainment purchases

Notes:
- $395 annual fee
- $300 annual Capital One Travel credit
- 10,000 bonus miles on each anniversary
- Premium travel benefits (lounge access, etc.)


7. AMEX PLATINUM
----------------
Earning Structure:
- 5x points on flights booked directly with airlines or through Amex Travel (up to $500,000 per calendar year, then 1x)
- 5x points on prepaid hotels booked through Amex Travel
- 1x point on all other purchases

Notes:
- $895 annual fee
- Extensive travel and lifestyle credits ($300 digital entertainment, $200 Uber, $200 airline fee, etc.)
- Premium lounge access and travel benefits
- Best for flights and Amex Travel hotel bookings only


8. AMEX BLUE CASH EVERYDAY
---------------------------
Earning Structure:
- 3% cash back at U.S. supermarkets (up to $6,000 per year, then 1%)
- 3% cash back on U.S. online retail purchases (up to $6,000 per year, then 1%)
- 3% cash back at U.S. gas stations (up to $6,000 per year, then 1%)
- 1% cash back on all other purchases

Notes:
- No annual fee
- Cash back as Reward Dollars (statement credit or Amazon)
- Up to $84 Disney Bundle credit annually ($7/month)
- Up to $180 Home Chef credit annually ($15/month)
- 0% intro APR for 15 months on purchases and balance transfers
